Recycling & Upcycling

The Recycling & Upcycling project focuses on educating communities about the importance of recycling and how to effectively reduce waste. Our goal is to empower individuals and businesses to transform waste materials into valuable resources, promoting sustainability and environmental awareness.

Through workshops and community events, we encourage everyone to think creatively about how to repurpose items and reduce their environmental impact.
